COMMON QUESTIONS

What version of Minecraft do I need?
EVBM PK CIV runs on Minehut and supports Minecraft Java Edition. Most recent versions work — just add evbm.minehut.gg and connect.
Is it free to play?
Yes — 100% free. Every rank, every course, every battle is earned through gameplay. No pay-to-win. The grind is the reward.
What is a parkour battle?
Two players face off. Player 1 attempts a jump. If they land it, Player 2 must match it. Miss and you lose a life. Three lives each — last one standing wins.
Is progress saved when I log off?
Absolutely. Your rank, achievements, and progress are permanently saved. Log off and come back whenever — your civilization waits.

WHAT EACH RANK UNLOCKS

Every rank opens new doors. Here's what's waiting for you.

NOOB
  • When you die, you go to prison
  • Collect tickets from the Ticket Temple
  • Use tickets to rank up to Pro
PRO
  • When you die, you rank down
  • Collect keys from the Key Temple
  • Use keys to rank up to Master
  • Access to the Build Area
  • Access to the Pro area
  • Unlocks commands: /steak & /ticket
MASTER
  • When you die, you become Pro
  • Access to the Legend Temples
  • No items needed to enter Legend Temples
  • Access to the Master area and arena
  • Unlocks commands: /goldencarrot & /key
CHAMPION
  • No weekly battle quota
  • Can promote and demote players
  • Become staff
